Calm dog grooming in Seattle

Grooming for anxious and reactive dogs.

Some dogs need less pressure, more time, and a groomer who will notice when a plan is not working. Sweet Beans is appointment-only and one-on-one, so anxious dogs are not moving through a crowded salon schedule.

Good fit

Who Calm Start is for

Calm Start is designed for dogs who shake, bark, freeze, pull away from tools, dislike feet or face handling, or have had a rough grooming history. It can also help dogs who simply need a slower first visit with a new person.

Approach

What changes

The appointment starts with a realistic comfort check. We may use shorter handling windows, quiet breaks, simpler goals, and pickup notes that explain what worked. If a full groom is not the kindest first step, we will say so.

What to expect

Clear limits, calm pacing

Grooming should still be safe for your dog and for the groomer. The goal is progress, not forcing every step in one visit.

Pricing

Calm Start starts at $150

Final pricing is confirmed at drop-off after seeing your dog's coat, size, and comfort level. If your dog needs veterinary support, sedation grooming, or a different environment, Sweet Beans may not be the right fit for that visit.
