Doodle grooming in Seattle

Practical grooming for doodles and curly coats.

Curly and mixed coats can be beautiful, but they need realistic maintenance. Sweet Beans focuses on clean prep, honest coat-condition conversations, and haircuts your dog can live comfortably in.

Coat care

Matting is handled honestly

If tangles are tight against the skin, brushing them out may hurt. In that case, a shorter reset can be the safer, kinder choice. Pricing is confirmed after seeing the coat in person.

Maintenance

The best haircut is sustainable

We can talk through coat length, brushing expectations, bath timing, and how often your dog should come in so the next visit is easier.

Good to know

Most doodles need a rhythm

Longer styles require more home brushing and more frequent grooming. Shorter styles can still look soft while being easier on the dog and the household schedule.
