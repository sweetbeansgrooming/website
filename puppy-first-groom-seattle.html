Puppy's first groom in Seattle

A gentle first grooming visit.

The first puppy appointment is less about a perfect haircut and more about building trust. Sweet Beans keeps the visit calm, short, and focused on the sounds, surfaces, and handling your puppy will need for life.

Puppy Intro

Small steps matter

Puppy Intro can include a puppy-safe bath, low-velocity drying, nails, light face and paw tidy, and gentle handling with breaks. The exact plan follows your puppy's comfort.

Expectations

Confidence beats perfection

Some puppies are ready for more, and some are learning how to stand, be touched, and hear tools. A positive first experience makes future grooms easier.

Before the visit

Set your puppy up well

Short car rides, gentle foot handling, brushing in tiny sessions, and calm praise all help. Bring vaccination and health context if anything is still in progress.

01

Arrive

Your puppy gets a calm handoff and a chance to settle.

02

Explore

We introduce water, dryer sound, table time, and handling slowly.

03

Tidy

Simple face, feet, nails, and coat care happen as comfort allows.

04

Repeat

Pickup notes help choose the right rhythm for the next visit.
