Senior dog grooming in Seattle

Gentler grooming for older dogs.

Senior dogs may need more patience, shorter standing time, and a practical haircut that keeps them comfortable between visits. Sweet Beans keeps senior grooming one-on-one and appointment-only.

Comfort

Built around the dog in front of us

Older dogs can have sore hips, lower stamina, vision changes, or less tolerance for long sessions. We plan around comfort and safety instead of chasing a perfect finish at any cost.

Planning

Simple maintenance helps

For senior coats, a slightly simpler trim or more regular Bath & Brush schedule can be kinder than waiting for tangles to become a big job.

Before you book

Tell us what has changed

Recent injuries, new medications, coughing, collapsing, seizures, or major behavior changes are important grooming context. Veterinary clearance may be needed for some dogs.
