Our Story

Care built on trust, one dog at a time.

Sweet Beans Grooming was created to offer thoughtful, detail-oriented care in a setting that feels personal and respectful, for both dogs and their people.

Independently Operated Seattle, WA

Grooming works best when it's built on trust. Every dog has their own personality, comfort level, and boundaries, and those are taken seriously here. The goal is to work with each dog, not rush them through an appointment, and to make grooming a positive experience they can feel good about.

What's Included

Thoughtful care, clearly offered

Services are designed to be straightforward and inclusive. Rather than charging separately for every small detail, packages are structured so clients know what's included upfront and what to expect at drop-off.

Small touches (like Sweet Bean Paw Care) reflect an attention to comfort and everyday practicality, especially for life in the Pacific Northwest.

A Personal Approach

Independently operated, intentionally small

This allows for clear communication, flexibility, and care that doesn't feel rushed or impersonal. Dogs are guided through the grooming process with patience, clear communication, and encouragement, rather than force.

When someone leaves their dog here, that trust matters. Each pet is treated with patience, respect, and the same consideration you'd want for a dog you love.
